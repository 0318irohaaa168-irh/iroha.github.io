Moist Convection in Giant Planets

I'm interested in atmospheric dynamics in giant planets.
In particular, I focus on how fundamental atmospheric processes—such as convection, condensation, precipitation, and evaporation—affect the thermal and dynamical structures of giant planet atmospheres.

My goal is to understand the underlying mechanisms governing these processes and to identify universal characteristics shared among giant planet atmospheres.
